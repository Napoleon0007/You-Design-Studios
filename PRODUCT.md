# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

South Africans who want premium custom-printed apparel and want it to feel like *theirs*, not a generic marketplace tee. They arrive with an idea (their art, their identity, their crew) and want to see it on a real garment fast. **Most arrive on a phone** — mobile is the primary surface, not an afterthought.

## Product Purpose

TRUEF Studios is a print-on-demand storefront + live 3D design studio for South Africa. It turns anyone into their own clothing brand: upload art, place it on premium tees/hoodies in a real-time 3D studio, order, get it printed and shipped locally. Success = a first-time visitor feels *"I can make something premium that's actually mine,"* and starts a design (enters the studio).

## Brand Personality

Premium streetwear with a self-made backbone. Three words: **bold, premium, self-made.** Voice is direct and empowering — "Be your own brand. You design it, you wear it, you own it." The all-caps TRUEF throw-up wordmark (Titan One, graffiti energy) carries the streetwear edge; the airy Lacoste-grade layout and restraint carry the premium. The tension between those two *is* the brand — never one without the other.

## Anti-references

- Printful / Redbubble / generic POD marketplaces — busy product grids, coupon banners, stock-photo trust badges, bargain-bin energy.
- Cheap "custom t-shirt printing" SEO sites.
- Over-busy POD admin dashboards bleaking into the storefront.
- AI-cream editorial-magazine restraint (display-serif + drop caps). TRUEF is street + premium, not literary.
- Anything that reads "discount."

## Design Principles

1. **The garment is the hero.** The site is the gallery wall; the customer's art is the colour. The interface stays quiet so the product pops.
2. **Premium through restraint + one living moment.** The colour-cycling 3D stage is the single alive flourish — disciplined, on-brand tones only, never busy or garish.
3. **Mobile-first, always legible.** Verified at 360/390/414 before desktop. Contrast and touch targets are non-negotiable (Luke's #1 rule).
4. **Show, don't tell.** Live 3D, real product, real motion — not promises or feature lists.
5. **Street × premium.** Bold wordmark and confident copy, framed by calm space. Hold the tension on every surface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1, large/display ≥3:1, on every backdrop the colour-cycle can land on.
- Reduced-motion alternative for the colour-cycle, parallax, and hero intro (partially implemented — keep it complete).
- Touch targets ≥44px; safe-area insets respected on mobile.
